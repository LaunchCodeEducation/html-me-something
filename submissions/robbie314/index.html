A Spiritual Journey

Tedda Boom Boom - the Beginning

I was born on March 6 to a passionate mother and an ambitious father. I was their firstborn...together and separately.
As I entered this realm, I knew my destiny. I just wasn't sure I wanted to stay on that course. I mean really.
This greatness in me just couldn't contain itself. I had an immediate relationship with God and understood
that faith would see me through anything.

A Blessed & Wild & Crazy Childhood

So much love! I received more love than my little heart could hold. My brother was born and then my sisters. A zillion cousins always around.

My grandmother was my rock. I could tell you I was a millionaire because I never wanted for anything.
I loved my little town and my friends. Hated the demons of the world. God helped me to diminish their existence.

Fasten Your Seatbelts - Turbulence

Okay, talk about going from sugar to sh*t. Maybe my security in my God kept me from doing my daily work to sustain a relationship.
I got cocky. Ungrateful. Rebellious. I took every wrong turn. Why? Because I could. And then in small moments, I would be reminded
who I was and whose I was. Only then, slowly, did I even think about coming back to center.

How much pain, hurt, anger, destruction could one soul take and actually survive? I was going to be the testament. No doubt.

Redemption - Renewal - Connection - Oneness

Why was I torturing myself when I knew from the moment I was born that greatness, happiness were me?
They were my destiny even before I was birthed. I decided it was time to be the I Am that I Am called me.
I became a mother and this being deserved to be connected to her Source, know her power.
Who else to teach her but the one whose heartbeat was her first song.
So for me and for her and for my world, I've stepped into my true self.
I no longer deny my talents and gifts. I am here to share them and express them to and with and for the world!

I do believe in miracles again! Be reminded that there is something greater at hand always working on your behalf.
God uses others to help us. Nous sommes bénis outre mesure!

Believe in miracles again!
