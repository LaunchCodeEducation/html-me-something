Debut in fall 1970

Originally offered as 2 door hardtop or convertible

Only lasted 5 years originally

Dodge stopped production in 1974

1978 it returned

Restyled in 1981

Ceased production in 1984

2006 Dodge Challenger concept was unveiled

2008 The return of the Dodge Challenger SRT8

2009 Dodge expanded the line to include SE & R/T
